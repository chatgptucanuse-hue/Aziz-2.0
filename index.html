Frequently Asked Questions

What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) improves your website's visibility on search engines to drive organic traffic.

How does Google Ads work?

Google Ads places your ads in search results and on the web; you bid on keywords and pay when users click (PPC).

How long does SEO take?

SEO is a medium- to long-term strategy; meaningful results often appear within 3–6 months depending on competition.
